what are we into

We have a mission. We intend to contribute to all of the sustainable development goals of the United Nations within one journey. Combine planetary integrity with human wellbeing; solve the problem of poverty and inequality with the use of technology. Machines have no intrinsic motivation to earn money and accumulate wealth. Self-sustainable infrastructures of autonomous machines can be programmed to serve the common good using distributed ledger technology (DLT, such as blockchain), artificial intelligence (AI) and autonomous assets.
