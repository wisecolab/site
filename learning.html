Learning

Targeted Training Programs
"Learning is the new skill. Imagination, creation and asking new questions are at its core --Sugata Mitra"
From middle schools to universities, from students to people working in the field; training and learning programs will be offered. Where possible, we adopt the methodology of self-organised learning ( the school in the cloud, developed by Prof. Sugata Mitra) to trigger the curiosity of trainees and enhance awareness about the possibilities that come with the technologies that are developed today and tomorrow. It enables us to reach remote areas and people from all over the world can join us. In India, we aim to include all districts and vernaculars.
Including girls and underprivileged youth is important to us. As some (or many) of the trainees will be first generation learners, they will not have to change their mental constructs. These youngsters can build them from scratch, or not build them as constructs at all.
