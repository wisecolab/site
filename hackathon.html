HACKATHON

Co-creating relevant solutions
"We cannot solve a problem by using the same kind of thinking we used when we created them-- Einstein"
Once a year, a Hackathon will be organizsed with 8 tracks that are relevant to the country. In 2019, up to 8 teams per track will be co-creating solutions relevant to (mainly) rural areas in India. Using our collective intelligence, we build on solutions that have an impact today. Teams will be challenged to come out of their comfort zone and experience the joy of letting go of mental constructs. We can already announce that Professor Sugata Mitra will spend a few days at the Hackathon to ask questions teams have never been asked before.
